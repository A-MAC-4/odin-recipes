Coffee and a Smoke

Full list of recipes

Why Should I Make this Recipe?

You shouldn't, but if you do its great.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbs of ground Coffee beans
  • 4 cups of Water
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Cream or Milk (optional)
  • Cigarette

Steps

  1. Grind beans in coffee grinder. If you don't have a coffee grinder just buy pre-ground beans
  2. Add beans to coffee filter in your coffee machine, add water to coffee machine
  3. Brew the coffee
  4. Pour coffee
  5. Go outside with coffee and your smoke
  6. Light your smoke
  7. Enjoy!
