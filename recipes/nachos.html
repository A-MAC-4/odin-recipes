Weekend Nacho Recipe

Full List of Recipes

Why Should I Make this Recipe?

This recipe is so simple which makes it a great option for a saturday night supper, or any night supper. Pair with your favorite beer some some good friends! You can't go wrong. Feel free to adjust the toppings to your taste. And to be honest, who even needs a nacho recipe. Oh well, here we go!

Ingredients

  • 1 large bag Tortilla Chips
  • 1 block of Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 medium jar of mild salsa
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 2 small jalapenos
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 1 medium bell pepper
  • 1 medium tomato
  • 1 green onion

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 f
  2. Prepare a baking sheet with parchment paper
  3. While oven preheats, chop all veggies into about half inch pieces. Set green onions aside
  4. Shred cheese
  5. Layer nachos, chips, cheese, veggies but leave out the green onion. Repeat until all ingredients are used
  6. Once oven is heated, place pan of nachos in oven for 10 minutes or until cheese starts to bubble
  7. Garnish with salsa, sour cream, and green onions
  8. Enjoy!
